About

A little information

Who We Are?

After moving to San Diego, I have experienced street surfaces be resurfaced, while the businesses slowly trickle in, laughter rise from the concrete walls and the welcoming hand of the neighbor. I’ve witnessed the community flourish with life at the local fairs and festivals. I started out with taking care of pets simply out of the joy of surrounding myself around the loved and equally loving pets. As I began to widen my perspective and understanding of the added family members, I felt that I wanted to give more to the San Diego community. I started out by growing within the pet community. I am starting off with providing services that are tailored to each walk, each visit to each family’s needs. I work on the foundation of supporting the family and providing for the member that might be void in the English vernacular, however he may have more than enough to liven a room with his smile or she will capture what words run absent of with her simple grace of greeting you at the door.

What We Do?

The Paws and Claws guarantees the best in-home pet care for our pet clients. Our pet care family will always ensure your pets' health and safety. We will give them the exercise, love and companionship they require to live long and healthy lives. Our goal is to provide you peace of mind, while always providing the best care. If you are unsatisfied with our services, we will provide our next service for you free of charge.

Why We Do It?

To start off, for our love for animals. The Paws and Claws is dedicated to seeing your loved family member have an extra spring to his step. We love caring for loved one while you are away and offer the best possible care.

Since When?

We have been taking care of your loved ones since a sunny afternoon on March 4th of 2004.

Pet Care

These are the words that describe our feelings, our philosophy, and our approach to safeguarding your pet’s happiness and welfare. And if these qualities are central to your decision about who you can trust with your beloved fur-baby, then you’ve definitely found the right people. Launched by a NAPPS Certified Professional Pet Sitter, every member of our team has a relentless passion for animals! We are all lifelong pet owners with plenty of knowledge and experience with furry family members. And our care for your pets will be full of the same TLC that we shower on our own spoiled pets! We strive to make sure that your beloved companions receive loving 1-on-1 care when you can't be at home with them. Our services include dog walking and pet sitting for dogs, cats, fish, birds, and most caged pets. You will have ONE PRIMARY pet sitter that you and your cherished pet can rely on and build a relationship with. A secondary pet sitter will step in only if your primary sitter is ill or out of town.

Pet Doctor

The Paws and Claws have been providing quality care for your loved ones since March 4th of 2004. Our mission is to provide the very best care for your best friend and each team member is well versed in handling emergency situations and are CPR and First Aid Certified. We hope you spend a few minutes browsing our website to learn about our practice, or better yet, come in to see it for yourself. We can't wait to meet you and your pet!

Pet Time

We treat your pet like family. The Paws and Claws provides all types of care for your pet. Our old-fashioned, hands-on approach includes loving and getting to know you and your pet. We exceed our clients’ expectations for the health care of their pets.

Pet Love

At The Paws and Claws, we believe in a safe and happy world where pets get their way every day. This means enabling your pet to live life to its fullest. So even if you’re away, with The Paws and Claws, your pet can still play! We believe a pet needs regular exercise in order to be happy and healthy, so we have designed a range of services to meet the varied needs. We also believe that your pet should be in a “free range”, stress-free environment with one-on-one care, which is why we provide in-home pet sitting where your pet will be free of the physical and emotional stress of a kennel, boarding or daycare facility or a “dog hotel”. So, when you’re away you’ll have the peace of mind knowing your pets can stay in the safety and comfort of your home and maintain their normal routine.
